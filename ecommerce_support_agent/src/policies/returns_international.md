# Regional and International Return Policies

**Source Docs:** docs/regional_returns.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-02-15

Return policies may vary depending on the shipping region and local consumer protection laws.

### European Union (EU) and UK
Under the EU/UK Right of Withdrawal, customers residing in these regions have the legal right to cancel and return their order for any reason within 14 days of receiving the final item in their order.
- They must be refunded the full cost of the item, including standard outbound shipping costs.
- The customer is responsible for the return shipping costs unless the item is defective.
- The 14-day statutory right overrides our standard 30-day policy if the item is returned within 14 days, but our 30-day policy still applies after the 14-day period expires.

### California (USA)
For customers residing in California, refunds must be processed within 7 business days of the returned item being scanned by the carrier. Additionally, California law requires that any gift card with a balance of less than $10.00 be redeemable for cash upon the customer's request.

### Cross-Border Returns
For all international shipments outside of North America and Europe:
- ABC does not provide prepaid return labels. Customers must arrange and pay for their own return shipping trackable method.
- Customs duties and import taxes paid at the time of purchase are strictly non-refundable by ABC. Customers must contact their local customs office for duty refunds.
