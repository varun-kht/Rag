# Marketplace Seller Policies

**Source Docs:** docs/seller_marketplace.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-07-12

ABC hosts third-party marketplace sellers. Items fulfilled by third-party sellers (i.e., `fulfillment_type: marketplace`) are subject to the individual seller's policies as well as ABC's A-to-Z Guarantee.

### Initiating Contact with Sellers
For any issues regarding a marketplace order, the customer must first contact the third-party seller directly through the portal and allow the seller 48 hours to respond. 
ABC support agents **cannot** intervene or issue refunds for marketplace orders until the 48-hour window has passed without a resolution.

### A-to-Z Guarantee
If 48 hours have passed and the marketplace seller has not responded or refused a standard policy-aligned resolution, the ABC agent can step in and file an A-to-Z Guarantee claim on behalf of the customer.
Once an A-to-Z Guarantee claim is opened, ABC will investigate and, if approved, issue a refund directly.

### Return Shipping for Marketplace
Marketplace sellers are not required to provide prepaid return labels unless the item arrived damaged or materially different from the description. If the return is discretionary, the customer pays return shipping to the seller's facility, not a ABC facility.
