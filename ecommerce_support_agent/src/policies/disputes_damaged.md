# Damaged or Incorrect Items

**Source Docs:** docs/disputes_damaged.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-03-25

If an item arrives damaged, defective, or materially different from what was ordered, the customer is entitled to a swift resolution without any out-of-pocket costs.

### Reporting the Issue
Damaged or incorrect items must be reported within 14 days of the marked `delivery_date`. If reported after 14 days, the standard 30-day return policy applies (the customer may return it, but may not be eligible for a free replacement).

### Resolution Protocol
When a customer reports a damaged or incorrect item within 14 days, the support agent should offer the following solutions:
1. A free replacement shipped immediately, with a prepaid label to return the damaged item.
2. A full refund to the original payment method, and a prepaid label to return the item.
For items valued under $25, the support agent may authorize a refund or replacement without requiring the item to be shipped back.

### Proof Requirements
Support agents have the discretion to ask for photos of the damage or the incorrect item before processing a replacement or refund, especially for items valued over $100. If the customer refuses to provide photos, they must still return the item using a standard label before a refund or replacement is dispatched.
