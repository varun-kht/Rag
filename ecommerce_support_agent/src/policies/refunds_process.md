# Refund Process and Payment Methods

**Source Docs:** docs/refunds_process.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-03-01

This policy outlines how and where refunds are issued when a customer is eligible for money back.

### Method of Refund
Refunds must be issued exclusively to the original method of payment used at checkout. We cannot issue refunds to a different credit card or alternative payment method to prevent fraud.
If the original payment method is no longer valid or active (e.g., closed account, expired card), the refund must still be initiated to that account, and the customer must contact their financial institution to retrieve the funds. 

### Processing Times
After a refund is initiated by our system, it takes approximately 3 to 5 business days for the funds to appear on the customer's bank statement. 
If the customer used a ABC Gift Card, the gift card balance is refunded instantly.

### Partial Refunds
In cases of missing parts, slight damage where the customer wishes to keep the item, or goodwill gestures, support agents are authorized to issue a partial refund. 
- A partial refund should not exceed 30% of the item's purchase price without managerial approval.
- Partial refunds are the final resolution for that specific issue. The customer cannot later request a full refund for the same problem.
