# Return Policy Exceptions

**Source Docs:** docs/returns_exceptions.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-08-01

While our standard 30-day return policy covers most items, there are several product categories that have strict exceptions due to health, safety, and regulatory reasons.

### 1. Final Sale Items
Any item explicitly marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase is non-returnable and non-refundable under any circumstances unless arrived damaged. This includes clearance merchandise and customized/personalized items.

### 2. Hygiene and Personal Care Items
For health and safety reasons, we **do not accept returns** on hygiene products if the factory seal has been broken or the item has been opened.
This policy applies to:
- Underwear and intimate apparel
- Swimwear (if the protective hygiene strip is removed)
- Cosmetics and skincare products
- Earrings and body jewelry
If a customer attempts to return an opened hygiene item, the request must be denied.

### 3. Perishable Goods
Perishable goods, including fresh food items, groceries, flowers, and temperature-sensitive products, **cannot be returned**.
If a perishable item arrives spoiled or damaged, the customer is entitled to a full refund or replacement, but they do not need to ship the original item back. The support agent should issue a refund immediately upon receiving photo evidence, or if the customer's history is in good standing.

### 4. Digital Goods and Software
Digital downloads, gift cards, and activated software licenses are strictly non-refundable once the activation code has been displayed or the download has commenced.
