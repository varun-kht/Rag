# Shipping and Delivery Options

**Source Docs:** docs/shipping_delivery.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-04-10

We offer several shipping options for our customers, with delivery times varying based on the selected method.

### Free Shipping Thresholds
Orders over $50 qualify for free Standard Shipping. The minimum purchase amount must be met after any discounts or promotions are applied, and before taxes.

### Shipping Methods
- **Standard Shipping:** 5-7 business days.
- **Expedited Shipping:** 2-3 business days. Note that expedited shipping fees are non-refundable if an item is returned for discretionary reasons.
- **Overnight Shipping:** 1 business day (order must be placed by 1:00 PM EST). 

### Late Deliveries
A delivery is considered "late" only if it arrives after the guaranteed expected delivery date provided at checkout.
If a guaranteed Expedited or Overnight shipment arrives late, the customer is entitled to a full refund of the shipping fee. 
If a Standard shipment arrives late, support agents may issue a $10 promotional credit as a goodwill gesture, but the order itself is not eligible for a full refund solely based on a late standard delivery if the item is still wanted.
