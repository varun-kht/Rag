# Order Cancellations

**Source Docs:** docs/cancellations.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-01-20

### Cancellation Window
Customers can cancel their order self-serve within 30 minutes of placing it without contacting support, as long as the order status is "placed" or "processing" and not yet "shipped".

### Post-Processing Cancellations
Once the order status moves to "shipped" or "in transit", the order cannot be cancelled under any circumstances. 
If a customer wishes to cancel after the items have shipped, they must wait for the order to arrive and initiate a standard return. 
Support agents cannot override the shipping status to cancel an order.

### Pre-Order Items
Pre-order items can be cancelled at any time before the stated release date. If the release date has passed and the item is in fulfillment, standard cancellation rules apply.

### System Cancellations
ABC reserves the right to cancel orders automatically if the payment is flagged as fraudulent or if the item is out of stock. In these cases, the customer must be notified by email and no charge will be posted to their account, or an immediate full refund will be issued.
