# Lost or Missing Packages

**Source Docs:** docs/shipping_lost.html
**Last Updated:** 2024-06-05

Occasionally, packages may be marked as "Delivered" but the customer cannot locate them, or tracking may stall.

### Tracking Stalled
If tracking has not updated for 7 consecutive days, the package is considered lost in transit. Support agents must immediately offer a replacement or a full refund at the customer's choice.

### Marked as Delivered but Not Received
If the tracking shows "Delivered" but the customer claims they did not receive it:
1. The customer must wait an additional 48 hours, as carriers sometimes scan packages early.
2. The customer should be advised to check with neighbors and in unusual spots around the home (e.g., side door, porch).
3. If the package is still missing 48 hours after the delivery scan, the support agent should issue a one-time replacement or a refund. We cannot issue a second replacement if the first replacement also goes missing.

### Freight Forwarders
We are not responsible for packages once they are successfully delivered to a freight forwarder address. Any claims of missing packages from a freight forwarded address must be denied, and the customer must take the issue up with their forwarding service.
